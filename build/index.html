Story

Thats how this story begins

Friendly Samoyeds are kind and loyal to their owner, but despite this they are very playful and sometimes like to do all sorts of dirty tricks. Our heroes are ICE and SNEZHA, one day they stole their owner’s car and went on a journey.

ICE often watched the Discovery Channel with his owner and his dream was to get to Hawaii with his girlfriend. Putting on a Hawaiian shirt and took the situation into his own paws and they set off on a hijacked car, because it was the case without preparation, as usually it happens, they got lost and ended up in Arctic.

Read more

By minting one ore few NFTs from this collection you can help them return home, accelerating their progress in the Decentraland metaverse. Their mission is to bring fun and make people smile. Therefore, it is very important to return them home, to their loved ones and without your help it will be impossible.

Since Samoyeds are very friendly and love to share everything they have with everyone around them, part of the funds raised with minting will go straight to help homeless animals who are left alone on the street without their owners.
